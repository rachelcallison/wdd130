About

Most DIY sites today feel like digital storefronts where every tutorial is hidden behind a paywall. I’m building CraftCraft, a social network that brings the 'maker spirit' back to its roots.

It’s a dedicated community hub where crafters showcase their work, share step-by-step tutorials, and list materials—all entirely for free. Unlike marketplaces, our platform is a true SNS designed for enthusiasts to meet, ask questions, and solve project hurdles together.

We’re moving away from 'selling' and back to 'sharing.' Our mission is to make high-level crafting accessible to every hobbyist, one free guide at a time. I’d love to show you how we’re turning DIY into a collaborative social experience—do you have a moment to chat?
